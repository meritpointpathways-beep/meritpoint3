## Find the right program without guessing

Choosing a program isn’t just about what sounds interesting — it’s about picking something that fits your **goal**, **timeline**, **budget**, and **job outcome**.

At **MeritPoint Pathways**, we help you compare options clearly so you can move forward with confidence.

---

## What we help you with

- **Program shortlisting** based on your background and goals  
- **School comparisons** (public/private options where applicable)  
- **Admissions readiness** (what you need and when)  
- **Timeline planning** for upcoming intakes  
- **Funding clarity** (OSAP / loans / cost categories)

---

## Common program directions we can explore

These are examples of directions (we’ll shortlist specific programs after understanding your goals):

- Skilled trades & technical training  
- Healthcare & support roles  
- Business & administration  
- Technology & IT pathways  
- Customer service, logistics, and operations

---

## How the process works

1) **Free consultation** — your goal + timeline + background  
2) **Options review** — we explain realistic pathways  
3) **Shortlist** — clear list of programs that fit your situation  
4) **Next steps plan** — documents, deadlines, and what to do first
